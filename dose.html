Dose of Growth

Life’s greatest transformations come through struggle. Growth is a painful yet necessary process, a fire that refines the strongest metals. You are not defined by your past failures, but by your ability to rise from them.

Every setback is a lesson. Every failure is fuel. The only question is—how will you use it? Your future self depends on the work you do today. Keep pushing.
